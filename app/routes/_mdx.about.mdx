# About

## Intention

Learning English is not easy. And studying idioms, that have real meanings than literal meanings, is harder.

I have always wanted to help people who have the prejudice "Idioms are difficult to learn or remember". That is why I created this web application.

## Features

This can provide things to everyone.

- **Idiom**
- **Brief meaning**
- **Full meaning**  
  The detailed meaning, and situation each idiom can describe.
- **Examples**  
  You can easily understand how to write it through good examples.
